What Is The Future Of Electric vehicle In India?

This situation of covid-19 has given a boost to electric vehicles, but the problem of charging station and budget is very big.

The government of India has taken several steps to promote electric vehicles. Such as subsidies and tax exemption from the government to those who buy electric vehicles.Government to offer subsidy to 60,000+ electric cars and buses.

To boost electric vehicle, the government aims to support subsidies for about 60,000+ electric passenger cars and buses, in addition to 1.5 million electric three and two-wheelers, Parliament was informed. The focus has also been on building an electric charge infrastructure, Road Transport and Highways Minister Nitin Gadkari told the Lok Sabha in a written reply.

The government is also promoting a company that makes electric vehicles, which has increased the investment in this area.
